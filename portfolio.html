A SOUR WORLD

Created October 2021

A Sour World is an Olivia Rodrigo themed game. An image of Olivia is the sprite and user would need to tilt their device to move Olivia through the obstacles to reach the the door. Those obstacles are other sprites such as black rectangles forming a mazed-path. This program used block-based coding and focused on the accelerometer feature.

A WHOLE NEW GUESSING WORLD

Created December 2021

A Whole New Guessing World is a number guessing game, providing both single and two-player runs. A random number will either be generated or inputted, then someone will have to guess the secret number. Users will be told if they should guess higher or lower. You can also choose the number of digits you'd like to work with. This game is block-based and focused on using for-loops.

DISC PERSONALITY QUIZ

Created February 2022

The DISC Personality Quiz provides eight questions with designated personalities depending on the answers that the user pics. Data is collected in the form of a spreadhseet and each round is documented in the tester screen that tallies everything. DISC stands for Dominant, Influence, Steady, Conscious.
